About Us

A Family Business Built on Trust & Honesty

Shore Point Automotive is a family-run auto repair shop in Brick, NJ. Owner Robert (Bob) Mattliano Sr. opened the shop over 16 years ago, and today works alongside his son, Bob Jr., continuing the tradition of honest, quality work at fair prices.

As a NAPA Auto Care Center, we combine the personal touch of a local shop with the resources and warranty backing of one of the most trusted names in auto care.
